Alexandr Golub

I'm a photographer and videographer based in Niagara Region, Ontario. My work spans weddings, portraits, commercial projects, and aerial photography — each one approached with the same care and intention.

I believe the best images come from genuine connection — understanding who you are and what this moment means to you. The camera is just the tool.
